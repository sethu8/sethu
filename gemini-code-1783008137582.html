What are the Sustainable Development Goals?

Adopted by all United Nations Member States in 2015, the SDGs provide a shared blueprint for peace and prosperity for people and the planet, now and into the future. They recognize that ending poverty must go hand-in-hand with strategies that build economic growth and address social needs while tackling climate change.
